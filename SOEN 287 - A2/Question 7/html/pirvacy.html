Privacy Policy

Hope for Paw Foundation is committed to protecting your privacy. We will not sell or distribute your personal information to third parties without your permission.

Your privacy is important to us. Any personal information provided through this form will be kept strictly confidential and will only be used for the purpose of facilitating the adoption process. We take all necessary precautions to protect your data and ensure it is stored securely. Rest assured, your details will never be shared with third parties without your consent.

The Hope for Paw Foundation will not be heldliable for any errors or issues that may arise during the adoption process. We strive to ensure all information is accurate and up-to-date, but cannot guarantee the outcome of any adoption or related process.
